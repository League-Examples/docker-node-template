The League of Amazing Programmers is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit teaching real coding and robotics skills to kids across San Diego — completely free, funded by donors and hosts like you.

We're growing. Our goal is a Tech Club within reach of every neighborhood in the county.

That means finding new places to hold class — a school, a church, a community center, or a company conference room after hours — and companies willing to open their doors to young engineers.

One warm introduction beats a hundred cold emails. Help us find our next classroom — and our next generation of engineers.
